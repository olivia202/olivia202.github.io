Avocado Toast

Back to What's Cookin

What you need:
bread, avocado, butter/olive oil

Optional:
lemon juice, red pepper flakes, pepper, salt

Instructions:
1. Remove avocado pit and mash flesh in a bowl.
2. Add lemon juice and salt to taste (optional)
3. Toast a slice of bread
4. Spread avocado mixture on toast and garnish with red pepper flakes and olive oil
5. ENJOY!
