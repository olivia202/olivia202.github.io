Garlic Noodles

Back to What's Cookin

What you need:
noodles, garlic, butter/olive oil, parmesian cheese

Optional:
asian sauce, green onions, sesame seeds

Instructions:
1. bring water to boil and cook 8 onces of noodles according to package. set aside
2. place 3tbps butter and up to 8 cloves garlic in pan (medium heat) and let simmer (5-7 min). stir often
3. add in the sauce (optional)
4. add prepared noodles and toss in 1/4 cup parmesian cheese (1-2 min)
5. serve topped with onions and sesame seeds (optional)
6. ENJOY!
