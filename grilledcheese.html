Grilled Cheese

Back to What's Cookin

What you need:
two slices of bread, cheese, butter/olive oil

Instructions:
1. before you start make sure your cheese is either grated or sliced
2. melt a slice of butter in a frying pan (medium heat) and place one slice of bread on top
3. pile cheese on top and cover with the second slice of bread
4. flip it over after 2-3 min or when the bottem is golden brown
5. ENJOY!
