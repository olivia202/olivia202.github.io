Smoothies

Back to What's Cookin

What you need:
frozen or fresh fruit (berries, banana, mango), milk (almond, coconut, soy, normal), juice, yogurt

Optional:
ice, vanila extract

Instructions:
1. take your chosen fruit and yogurt (optional) and place it in a blender
2. add the juice/milk until it covers about half of the fruit
3. blend!
4. add vanila and or ice to taste and blend again (optional)
5. ENJOY!
